About Our Services

We offer dinner takeaway services in Poochiyur, NSN Palayam, Coimbatore. Our daily dinner menu will be shared via WhatsApp, and you can place your order by responding to the message. Ensure you receive confirmation from Magic Pan that your order is confirmed. You can collect your food takeaway from our home office at the designated time provided in the message. Payment can be made via UPI or cash. For any queries or specific food requests, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance, and we'll be happy to assist you.
